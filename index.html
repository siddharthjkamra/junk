What is Junk Framework?


A CSS Framework that allows you to build front of websites with modern designs and fast speed.

Team

Junk is maintained by some people. We (there's no "We" actually) are always looking for people who could contribute and improve this Framework. You are invited, only requirements are HTML, CSS and JS.

History

This started on May 31, 2022. It is at initial stage and we'll make it an advanced framework. I do not know what is the future of Junk but we would always listen to users suggestions and try to keep it update according to new traditions.

Focus

Our main focus is to keep it clean, lightweight and easy to use. Clean code and clean design both are our main target. We won't use any library and this should be made of pure CSS and JS.
